FAQ

Questions answered.

What's included in the free trial? +
You get a full server with the same panel, plugins, and features as a paid plan. The only limitations are that your server sleeps after 30 minutes of inactivity and the trial runs for 5 days. No credit card required.
Can I keep my world when I upgrade? +
Yes! Your world transfers automatically when you upgrade from trial to a paid plan. Everything — plugins, configs, player data — stays exactly as you left it.
What version of Minecraft can I run? +
We support all major versions from 1.8 to the latest 1.21.4. You can choose Paper, Spigot, Vanilla, Fabric, Forge, or Purpur — all available from the control panel.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Absolutely. Cancel from your dashboard at any time. Your server stays active until the end of the billing period, then deletes after 7 days with no charges.
How fast will my server be ready? +
Instantly. From payment to a live server is under 60 seconds. Free trial servers are also deployed instantly.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Payments are secure and we never store your card details.
